Employee Handbook

Company Name: ABC Technologies

Welcome Message
Welcome to ABC Technologies. This handbook provides important company policies, employee guidelines, workplace expectations, and benefits information.

1. Office Working Hours
- Monday to Friday
- 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Saturday and Sunday are weekly offs.

2. Dress Code
- Employees should maintain professional appearance.
- Smart casuals are allowed on Fridays.

3. Employee Benefits
- Health insurance
- Paid leave
- Performance bonuses
- Learning and development programs
- Employee wellness initiatives

4. Code of Conduct
Employees are expected to:
- Maintain professionalism
- Respect colleagues
- Protect confidential company data
- Follow organizational policies
- Maintain ethical behavior

5. Information Security
- Sharing confidential company information is prohibited.
- Employees must use company-approved systems only.

6. Work From Home Guidelines
- Employees must maintain internet connectivity during work hours.
- Availability during meetings is mandatory.

7. IT Support
For technical support contact:
itsupport@abctech.com

8. HR Support
For HR-related queries contact:
hrsupport@abctech.com

9. Learning and Development
Employees are encouraged to participate in:
- Technical trainings
- Certification programs
- Leadership workshops

10. Workplace Safety
Employees must follow:
- Emergency evacuation procedures
- Workplace safety guidelines
- Security compliance rules

11. Performance Management
Performance reviews are conducted every 6 months.

12. Anti-Harassment Policy
ABC Technologies maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward workplace harassment and discrimination.

13. Employee ID Card
Employees must carry ID cards inside office premises at all times.
