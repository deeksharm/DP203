Attendance Policy Document

Company Name: ABC Technologies
Department: Human Resources

Introduction
This attendance policy defines employee attendance expectations, login procedures, work hours, and disciplinary rules.

1. Daily Attendance
- Employees must mark attendance daily using company-approved systems.

2. Working Hours
- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
- Employees are expected to complete minimum 9 working hours daily.

3. Late Attendance
- Login beyond 15 minutes from shift start time is considered late attendance.
- Repeated late attendance may lead to HR warning.

4. Break Policy
- Employees are allowed one lunch break and short refreshment breaks.

5. Work From Home Attendance
- Remote employees must update attendance through HR portal.
- Availability during working hours is mandatory.

6. Unauthorized Absence
- Absence without manager approval is considered unauthorized.
- Unauthorized absence may result in salary deduction or disciplinary action.

7. Biometric Attendance
- Employees working from office must use biometric attendance systems.

8. Overtime
- Overtime requires manager approval.

9. Attendance Review
- HR reviews attendance reports monthly.

10. Public Holidays
- Attendance is not required on company-declared holidays.

11. Leave and Attendance Integration
- Approved leaves are automatically adjusted in attendance records.

12. Employee Responsibilities
Employees must:
- Maintain punctuality
- Inform managers in case of emergencies
- Follow attendance procedures
