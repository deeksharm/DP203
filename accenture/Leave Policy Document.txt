Leave Policy Document

Company Name: abc Technologies
Department: Human Resources

Introduction
This leave policy explains the leave rules, leave types, approval process, and employee responsibilities related to leave management.

1. Casual Leave
- Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves per year.
- Casual leaves are intended for personal work or emergencies.
- Casual leave should be applied in advance whenever possible.

2. Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to 10 sick leaves per year.
- Medical certificates may be required for leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.
- Sick leave is intended only for health-related issues.

3. Privilege Leave
- Privilege leave can be earned and carried forward up to 30 days.
- Unused privilege leave beyond the limit may lapse according to company policy.

4. Maternity Leave
- Female employees are eligible for maternity leave as per company policy and applicable labor laws.

5. Paternity Leave
- Male employees are eligible for 5 days of paternity leave.

6. Leave Approval Process
- Leave requests should be submitted through the HR portal.
- Reporting managers are responsible for approving or rejecting leave requests.
- Emergency leaves should be informed immediately through email or phone call.

7. Work From Home
- Work from home requests require manager approval.
- Employees must remain available during working hours.

8. Public Holidays
- Employees are eligible for company-declared public holidays.

9. Leave Without Pay
- Leave without pay may apply if leave balance is exhausted.

10. Attendance Impact
- Unapproved absence may impact payroll and performance evaluation.
